Sweet Ruby's was founded to honor my late grandmother after her passing. Using recipes that have been handed down over three generations, Sweet Ruby's is known for their custom cakes, cupcakes and dessert tables. Started in as home based business located in Nashville, Tennessee, Sweet Ruby's is currently looking to expand using shipping of select featured products.
